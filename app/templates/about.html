About

Opening

As a self-taught developer breaking into tech, I know firsthand how overwhelming it can be to figure out which skills employers actually want. That's why I built this tool - to help job seekers cut through the noise and focus their learning on what's truly in demand in their local market.

My transition story

After discovering that programming perfectly blends my love for both creative problem-solving and technology, I dove into self-directed learning through resources like FreeCodeCamp, The Odin Project, and ultimately Harvard's CS50 intro to computer science course.

Technical approach and skills

I chose Flask for its flexibility, integrated Selenium for reliable web scraping, and used Google Charts to make the data actionable and visually clear
